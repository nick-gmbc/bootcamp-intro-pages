Bootcamp - introduction to software development

Who is it for?

Anyone with some coding experience think a career in software development

Why do it

  • Get skills
  • Build a Portfolio
  • Meet great people
    • People from industry
    • Lecturers from College
    • Enthusiatic committed learners aka your group

What will be covered?

  • Client side coding in HTML and CSS
  • Javescript to add interactivity
  • Client side coding
  • CV and interview skills
  • Visits and meetings with local employers

How will be covered?

We realise that particpants in this course will have very different levels of experience

There will be introductory lectures. However, the majority of your time will be spent developing your technical and employability skills, through projects, from where you are utilising

  • The supporting lecturers
  • Supporting employers
  • Online resources
  • each other...
as resources.
